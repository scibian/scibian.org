--- title: Scibian launch event description: > Scibian launch event created: !!timestamp '2016-09-30 10:00:00' --- {% mark excerpt -%} Scibian launch event took place on the 30th of September 2016 in Paris. {%- endmark %} Scibian [launch event](https://www.eventbrite.fr/e/billets-scibian-linux-pour-lingenierie-scientifique-27404072318) took place on the 30th of September 2016 in Paris.
